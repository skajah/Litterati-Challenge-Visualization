About

This website aims to visualize the amount of litter that was picked up in the Forest Park suburb of Chicago, IL.
The majority of the data is from 2019, but there is some from 2018 and 2020 as well.
The data comes from http://litterati.org, which allows users to record the litter they have picked up.
